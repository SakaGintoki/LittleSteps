**LittleSteps** is a comprehensive Parenting SuperApp designed to assist parents in every step of their child's growth. Built with modern Android technologies (Jetpack Compose), it integrates AI assistance, e-commerce, caregiver services, and social impact features into a single, intuitive platform.

---

## App Overview

LittleSteps aims to be the ultimate companion for parents by providing:
* **AI Parenting Assistant:** Instant answers to parenting questions.
* **Marketplace:** A curated shop for baby and mother needs.
* **E-Sitter:** Easy booking for experienced babysitters.
* **Daycare Locator:** Find trusted childcare facilities nearby.
* **Social Impact:** A platform to donate to families in need.

## Key Features

### 1. Little AI
Powered by advanced language models, **Little AI** acts as a 24/7 virtual assistant. Parents can ask questions about nutrition, sleep patterns, or general health and receive instant, empathetic responses.

### 2. Shop (Marketplace)
A complete e-commerce module designed for parents:
* Browse categories (Milk, Diapers, Strollers, Vitamins).
* View product details and pricing.
* Integrated cart and checkout system.

### 3. E-Sitter Service
Find and book professional caregivers easily:
* Browse profiles of babysitters.
* View ratings, experience, and hourly rates.
* Direct booking interface.

### 4. Daycare Locator
Location-based search for childcare centers:
* View photos and facilities (AC, Meals, Playground).
* Check pricing and operating hours.
* "Near Me" filter support.

### 5. Care & Donate
A dedicated section for social responsibility, allowing users to donate to various campaigns (Education, Health, Disaster Relief) directly from their app balance.
